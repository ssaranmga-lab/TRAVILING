My Story

Hello! I'm Digital Saran, a passionate travel blogger, photographer, and storyteller based in Dubai, UAE. My journey began in 2015 when I took my first solo trip to Thailand, which ignited an insatiable wanderlust that has led me to explore over 35 countries across 6 continents.

What started as a personal travel journal has evolved into a platform where I share detailed travel guides, photography tips, and authentic stories from around the world. I believe that travel is not just about visiting famous landmarks but about immersing yourself in different cultures, connecting with locals, and discovering those hidden gems that make each destination unique.

My Travel Philosophy

I approach travel with curiosity, respect, and an open mind. My goal is to experience destinations authentically, beyond tourist hotspots, and to share these experiences in a way that inspires others to explore the world responsibly.

Whether it's hiking through remote mountain villages in Nepal, sampling street food in Bangkok, or photographing the northern lights in Iceland, I seek experiences that challenge perspectives and create lasting memories.

Professional Background

Before becoming a full-time travel blogger, I worked in digital marketing for 5 years, which gave me valuable skills in content creation, photography, and social media management. This background has been instrumental in building my travel blog and creating engaging content that resonates with readers.

My work has been featured in several travel publications, and I've collaborated with tourism boards and travel brands around the world. I'm also a member of the Professional Travel Bloggers Association and committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity in my content.

Photography & Storytelling

Photography is an integral part of my travel experience. I believe that images have the power to transport viewers to different places and inspire them to explore the world. I shoot primarily with a Sony Alpha camera and occasionally with my iPhone 14 Pro for spontaneous moments.

Beyond the technical aspects of photography, I focus on capturing authentic moments, cultural nuances, and the human connections that make travel so enriching. My goal is to tell visual stories that evoke emotion and curiosity.

Sustainable Travel

As a traveler who has witnessed the impact of tourism on local communities and environments, I'm committed to promoting sustainable and responsible travel practices. This includes supporting local businesses, minimizing environmental footprint, respecting cultural norms, and encouraging meaningful interactions with local communities.

I believe that travel, when done mindfully, can be a force for positive change in the world, fostering cross-cultural understanding and supporting local economies.

My Travel Timeline

A chronological journey through some of my most memorable travel experiences.

1

2015: First Solo Trip

Thailand became my gateway to the world of travel. Explored Bangkok, Chiang Mai, and the islands, discovering a passion for street photography and local cuisine.

2

2017: European Adventure

Backpacked through 8 European countries in 3 months. Highlights included hiking in the Swiss Alps, exploring ancient ruins in Greece, and experiencing the vibrant culture of Spain.

3

2019: South America Expedition

Trekked to Machu Picchu, explored the Amazon rainforest, and danced tango in Buenos Aires. This journey deepened my appreciation for biodiversity and indigenous cultures.

4

2021: Moving to Dubai

Made Dubai my home base, using it as a strategic location to explore the Middle East, Africa, and Asia. Started focusing more on sustainable travel and cultural immersion.

5

2023: Present & Future

Currently exploring hidden gems around the world and sharing authentic travel experiences. Planning expeditions to Antarctica and the Silk Road for the coming years.
