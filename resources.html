Ultimate Packing Lists

Tropical Destination Essentials

Everything you need for beaches, jungles, and island hopping in warm climates.

Clothing

  • 4 Lightweight t-shirts/tank tops
  • 2 Shorts/skirts
  • 1 Light pants (for temples/mosquito protection)
  • 2 Swimsuits
  • 1 Light jacket or sweater (for evening or AC)

Sun Protection

  • 1 Reef-safe sunscreen (SPF 30+)
  • 1 Sunglasses with UV protection
  • 1 Wide-brimmed hat
  • 1 After-sun lotion or aloe vera gel

Beach Essentials

  • 1 Quick-dry towel
  • 1 Waterproof phone case
  • 1 Snorkel set (if not renting)

City Explorer Essentials

Perfect for urban adventures, museum hopping, and cultural experiences in cities worldwide.

Clothing

  • 3 Versatile tops (mix of casual and dressy)
  • 2 Pants/jeans
  • 1 Dress/nice outfit (for restaurants/shows)
  • 1 Light jacket or cardigan
  • 1 Comfortable walking shoes

City Essentials

  • 1 Anti-theft daypack or crossbody bag
  • 1 Portable phone charger
  • 1 City map or offline maps app
  • 1 Reusable water bottle

Tech & Documents

  • 1 Travel adapter
  • 1 Camera with extra memory cards
  • 1 Passport and copies
